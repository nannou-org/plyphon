glide

The control family. One synth plays a looping melody and is never restarted - the host just retunes two special parameters each beat. freq is a LagControl, so a /n_set glides to the new pitch (a one-pole de-zipper, one step per control block) and the saw lead slides between notes. trig is a TrigControl, seen for exactly one control block then reset to 0; gated against noise it makes a short percussive tick at each onset.
